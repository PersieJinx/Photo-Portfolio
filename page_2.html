Project 1: The Light as a Metaphor

This project is meant to explore metaphorical visuals of light. Different qualities of light can convey vastly different moods and meanings, and can be seen in so many different settings. It can highlight your view or something, or it silhouette and obscure your view. It can create it's own images in the form of a shadow, or notably through a camera as all of these pictures were taken. It can play with your eyes and confuse you interpretation of what you are seeing. It can represent a glimmer of hope in the darkness, or conversely represent the power and influence of something.
