Project 2: Self Portrayal/Portraiture

In my protraiture, I tried to make the photos feel more candid and natural. It offers a look into someone else's life, and documents the way they interact with the world around them, offering multiple different views. Climbing a tree and smiling into the camera, and two candid photos, one taken as he was walking away, and one of a distracted reflection. In exploring self portraiture, I wanted to show a view more centered on the way I see myself, rather than the way I am viewed by others. Since so much of my time is spent on art, I took a self portrait of myself I had previously drawn and snapped a photo of that, framed by my hands holding the sketchbook. The other views of myself show the ways I physically might look at myself; in a mirror, through a camera, or simply looking down at my shoes.
