Project 4: Personal Project

Photos of flowers around Corvallis on a rainy spring day. As a set, this project is meant to show all the different blooms coming to life with the spring, as well as convey the vibrant colors of the flowers in contrast to the cold, gray day. When all the people are inside, trying to avoid the dreary cold, the flowers are out just the same.
